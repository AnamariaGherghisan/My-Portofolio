While I may not have any direct experience in this field, my previous roles as a packer/picker in a factory, a warehouse operative,assembly operative, machinist and valeter (car cleaner) have all equipped me with valuable skills such as attention to detail,problem-solving, and the ability to work well under pressure.

I also have a educational background in mass-media and journalism in university and laboratory technician in high-school, which has honed my communication and creativity skills that will come in handy for designing aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly interfaces.
